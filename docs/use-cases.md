# DeepGuard — Use Cases

Real-world problems DeepGuard helps address.

## Newsroom & Journalism Verification

Before publishing video evidence, journalists and fact-checkers can run it through DeepGuard to flag whether it shows signs of manipulation.

## Content Platform Moderation

Platforms that accept video uploads can use DeepGuard as a screening layer to flag potential deepfakes for human review.

## Fraud & Impersonation Defense

Security and fraud teams face a growing threat from synthetic video used in impersonation and social-engineering attacks. DeepGuard provides a technical check on video authenticity.

## Research & Education

DeepGuard is a clear, documented reference implementation of video deepfake detection — useful for students and researchers studying ResNeXt + LSTM architectures and temporal video forensics.

## Custom Detection Deployments

The project ships with a training pipeline and documented datasets, so teams can retrain or fine-tune the model on their own data and deploy a detection service tailored to their needs.
